Wellness for Women & Children Affected by Gender-Based Violence

Loyce Akoromit Foundation (LAFO) is a Christian community-based organization empowering women, children, and youth through psychosocial support, skills training, education, and advocacy in Ngora District, Uganda.

Support Our Work

Our Mission & Vision

Vision

A society with healthy, empowered and productive women.

Mission

To build the capacity of women, children, and youth through income generation, skills training, education, and living harmoniously with God and their environment.

Core Values

Teamwork · Transparency · Integrity · Accountability · Christ-centeredness

Why We Exist

In Kobuin Sub-county, Ngora district, gender-based violence remains widespread — including domestic violence, sexual assault, and emotional abuse. Many women and children face discrimination, neglect, and lack of awareness of their own rights. LAFO exists to break this cycle.

Our Programs

Holistic interventions to restore hope, dignity, and self-sufficiency.

🧠 Psychosocial Support & Mental Wellness

Structured counseling, psychotherapy, support groups, prayer partnerships, games and sports, and safe transitional housing.

📚 Education & Child Protection

Scholarship scheme (200 per year), mentoring life skills, school structure strengthening, and stakeholder sensitization.

💼 Economic Empowerment (IGAs)

Enterprise skills training, apprenticeships, small-scale farming, and income-generating activities for women and youth.

⚖️ Advocacy & Legal Protection

Raising GBV awareness, engaging policymakers, building advocacy networks, and legal strategies to protect survivors.

Our Key Objectives

Transform Attitudes

Change community beliefs and practices around GBV through education and social change programs.

Access to Services

Provide psychosocial support, legal aid, shelter, and IGAs for 300+ women and youth by 2027.

Advocate for Justice

Push for policies and laws that protect survivors and hold perpetrators accountable.

Our Approach

🏘️ Community-led approach 🚪 Door-to-door household empowerment 🗣️ Social Behavior Change & Communication (SBCC)
